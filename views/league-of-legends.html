League of Legends

One of the org's oldest and most successful teams, Fnatic's Valorant team is a powerhouse in the esport. From winning the first ever Valorant World Championship in 2011 to seven European split titles, the team still competes at the highest levels both in Europe and internationally, consistently vying for the title in Europe and beating out big international names to make deep runs at Worlds.
