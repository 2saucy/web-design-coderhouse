Valorant

The Valorant squad of Legit Ones is a force to be reckoned with in the world of esports, thanks to their incredible triumph in the inaugural Valorant World Championship in 2011. Alongside an exceptional record of seven South American split titles, this team consistently competes at the highest echelons of the game, both domestically and on the international stage, leaving a lasting impact with their phenomenal achievements.
